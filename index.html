About

I am Bhargab Das, currently a PhD researcher (supervised by Dr. Aprameyo Pal) at the Harish-Chandra Research Institute. I earned my B.Sc. (Hons.) in Mathematics and M.Math from ISI Kolkata.

My work lies in algebraic number theory and arithmetic geometry, with interests in properties of p-adic L-functions, Selmer groups associated to automorphic motives, p-adic Galois representations etcetra.

Email: bhargabdas[at]hri.res.in, bhargabdas.math[at]gmail.com
